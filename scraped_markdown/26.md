                Technology at UBS in a nutshell

### Enabled engineers

UBS has more engineers and developers than many technology companies, and we’re committed to a culture of engineering excellence.[
                Learn moreabout our Distinguished Engineers](/global/en/our-firm/what-we-do/technology/2021/distinguished-engineers.html)### Eye on AI? So do we.

We aim to become an AI-first institution where all benefit from AI tooling, and where we are building AI-powered capabilities to maximize impact for our clients, employees and shareholders in a responsible and sustainable way.[
                Learn moreabout Artificial Intelligence](/global/en/our-firm/what-we-do/technology/artificial-intelligence.html)### Going greener? We’re all in!

With thousands of applications running across our global businesses, we know that one of the next steps in their evolution is using greener development principles and reducing our carbon footprint. And we practice what we preach.[
                Learn moreabout Green Software Foundation](/global/en/media/display-page-ndp/en-20220407-green-software-foundation.html)### Hacking today and tomorrow

A decade of commitment, a decade of engineering change. See how we’re cultivating the talent and creativity of our technologists.[

                What you gain

So, we offer competitive benefits and rewards to all our employees so they stay healthy and enjoy a great career. Work with cutting-edge tech and agile practices on a global scaleBuilding some seriously cool AI-powered tools that everyone can benefit fromJoin a culture of continuous learning and developmentBenefit from our Distinguished Engineers frameworkMake use of our internal mobility possibilitiesHybrid working arrangementsInsurance coverage and wellbeing programsPension plans and an employee stock purchase planPaid leave to volunteer for community and environment40+ employee networks regularly communicating and hosting eventsCareer Comeback Program – support when you re-enter corporate world##
