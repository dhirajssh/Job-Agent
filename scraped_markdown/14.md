Contribute to the design and development of machine learning technologies at Adobe, collaborating with cross-functional teams and focusing on Generative AI.
## Your Role:

Key responsibilities include:

- Contributing to the design and development of Machine Learning technologies for text, image, audio, and video, with a focus on Generative AI.

- Working closely with engineers, researchers, and product managers to implement ML solutions for Adobe products.

- Collaborating with cross-functional teams, including machine learning engineers, research engineers, and product management, to contribute to new ML features.

- Building models leveraging large data.

- Preprocessing, cleaning, organizing, and evaluating datasets as needed for model training and evaluation.

- Evaluating model performance and improving accuracy.

- Maintaining detailed documentation of data processing, model development, testing, and integration.

### About You:

The ideal candidate will have:

- BS degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience required.

- Proficiency in one or more programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++.

- Experience with ML frameworks such as SparkML, TensorFlow, or PyTorch.

- Knowledge and experience with the entire ML training pipeline (data collection, training, and evaluation).

- Experience in Large Language Models (LLM), Transformer Models, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computer Vision, or Data Science.

- Good verbal and written communication skills.

- Good time management and organizational skills.

- Self-motivated, self-starter, quick learner, independent, and a love of learning.

### Compensation & Benefits:

- The U.S. pay range for this position is $85,500 -- $163,500 annually, with pay varying by work location and job-related knowledge, skills, and experience.

- Non-sales roles include base salary and short-term incentives in the form of the Annual Incentive Plan (AIP).

- Certain roles may be eligible for long-term incentives in the form of a new hire equity award.

- Additional benefits typically include healthcare coverage, paid time off, employee assistance programs, and retirement plans.

### Training & Development:

- Ongoing mentorship and access to resources are provided, with opportunities to expand knowledge of advanced machine learning techniques.

- Employees are encouraged to contribute ideas, explore new approaches, and continuously improve processes and models.

### Career Progression:

This entry-level role offers professional growth and development, with mentorship from senior ML and Research engineers and opportunities to advance within Adobe’s engineering and product teams.
### How to Apply:

- Submit your application as instructed in the job posting.

- Adobe will consider qualified applicants with arrest or conviction records for employment in accordance with state and local laws and “fair chance” ordinances.

- For roles open in Colorado, the application window will remain open until at least the stated date and time in Pacific Time, in compliance with pay transparency regulations.

- Massachusetts applicants are protected from lie detector test requirements.
